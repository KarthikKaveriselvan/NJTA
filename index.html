Mission

Jersey Tamil Academy was founded to create an interesting platform for children and young adult students living in the Garden State of New Jersey to learn - Read, Write and Converse - Tamil, an ancient South Indian Language that has a great history and rich literature. The goal of our school is to continue and revive Tamil amongst our next generation as language is the eye to the culture and make our children feel proud of our great Tamil language.

Motto

Our school’s motto is to pass our longest surviving classical language and culture on to our successive generations.

Our Thiruvalluvar Tamil School was state registered in New Jersey as a non-profit organization (since 2011) and also Federal Tax Exempt under 501(c)3 with IRS (since 2013).

School

The singular focus of our school is children’s Tamil learning - to Read, to Write and to Converse. In addition to teaching Tamil, we also promote Tamil culture by engaging in cultural activities such as Dance, Drama, Music and any art form based on Tamil language.
